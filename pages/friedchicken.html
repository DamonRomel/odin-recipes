Fried Chicken

a dish of fried crispy chicken pieces coated with seasoned flour

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. coat chicken in flour
  2. heat vegetable oil for frying
  3. carefully drop chicken into the oil
  4. take the chicken out when its golden brown
  5. put salt on the chicken while its hot
