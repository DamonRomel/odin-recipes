Birria Tacos

Shredded beef tacos with a spicy savory sauce topped with onions and cilantro

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Cilantro
  • White Onion
  • Sauce Stuff
  • Corn Tortilla

Steps

  1. cook beef
  2. make sauce while beef is cooking
  3. Shred the beef
  4. chop onions and cilantro
  5. put beef,onions,and cilantro on tortilla
