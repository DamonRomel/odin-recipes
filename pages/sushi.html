Sushi

a Japanese dish that features raw fish wrapped in seaweed annd rice, served with Salmon Roe on top with wasabi and soy sauce on the side

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Cucumber
  • Rice
  • SeaWeed
  • rice wine
  • Wasabi
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salmon Roe

Steps

  1. cook rice
  2. cut fish while rice is cooking
  3. lay rice out on the rolling mat
  4. place seaweed over the rice
  5. place fish on the seaweed
  6. roll the sushi up
  7. top with salmon roe
  8. cut the roll
  9. pour soy sauce and plate wasabi
